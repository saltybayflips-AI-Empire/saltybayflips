Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SaltyBayFlips LLC ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our websites (including saltybayflips.github.io) and our publishing channels on Pinterest, including Aesthetic Spaces Co, Luxe Living Daily, and Fuzzy Frames AI (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to our website. Our site does not use analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or login systems. If you email us, we retain your email only to respond to your inquiry.

2. Pinterest API usage

We use the Pinterest API solely to operate our own Pinterest business accounts. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Publish and schedule pins to our own boards;
  • Retrieve analytics (impressions, saves, clicks) about our own pins and boards.

We do not access, store, or share data about any other Pinterest users. Our Pinterest API app has no end users other than SaltyBayFlips LLC itself.

Outbound links to merchants may be tagged so that we receive a commission on qualifying purchases. We do not receive or store any information about your purchases — any transaction occurs entirely on the merchant's site under their own privacy policy.

4. Cookies and tracking

Our website does not set cookies and does not fingerprint visitors. Third-party platforms where we publish content (Pinterest, Amazon, etc.) have their own cookie and tracking practices governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Data retention and security

Because we do not collect visitor information, we have no personal visitor data to retain or secure. Internal operational data (our own Pinterest analytics) is stored locally on SaltyBayFlips LLC systems and is not shared with third parties.

7. Your rights

If you are a resident of the EU, UK, California, or another jurisdiction granting privacy rights, you may contact us to request confirmation of whether we hold any information about you. Because we do not collect visitor data, such requests will typically be answered in the negative.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
